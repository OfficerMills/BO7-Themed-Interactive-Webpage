How to Earn Your Beta Code

Step 1: Link Your Accounts

Connect your Twitch and Activision accounts to start earning rewards

+

Account Linking Requirements

To participate in the Black Ops 7 Early Access Beta program, you must link your Twitch account to your Activision account. This connection allows you to earn Twitch Drops while watching eligible streams.

Step-by-Step Linking Process:

  1. Open your web browser and sign in to your Twitch account
  2. In the same browser window/tab, sign in to your Activision account (must be on PC)
  3. Navigate to the Account Linking section in your Activision account settings
  4. Select "Link with Twitch Account" and continue
  5. You'll be redirected back to Twitch to complete the authorization process
  6. Confirm the link by checking both accounts show the connection

Important: Both accounts must be logged in within the same browser for the linking process to work correctly.

Step 2: Watch Eligible Streams

Watch participating streamers for required time to earn drops

+

Watch Time Requirements

To earn your Black Ops 7 Early Access Beta Code, you must watch eligible streamers on Twitch for a minimum of one continuous hour.

How to Participate:

  • Look for streams with the "Drops Enabled" indicator
  • This callout confirms you're officially earning progress toward rewards
  • Watch time must be continuous - switching streams may reset your progress
  • Ensure you're logged into both Twitch and have linked accounts
  • Stream must be live (not VODs or reruns) to count toward watch time

Tracking Your Progress

You can monitor your drop progress through your Twitch account. Look for the drops notification that will appear when you've met the time requirements.

Step 3: Claim Your Rewards

Collect and activate your earned beta codes and rewards

+

Reward Claiming Process

Once you've completed the required watch time, you'll need to manually claim your rewards through Twitch.

Claiming Steps:

  1. Go to your Twitch Inventory (accessible through your profile menu)
  2. Find your earned Black Ops 7 Beta drop
  3. Click "Claim" to activate the reward
  4. You have 48 hours from earning to claim the drop

In-Game Activation

  • Claimed rewards may take up to 24 hours to appear in-game
  • Restart your game client if rewards don't appear immediately
  • Check your email for beta code delivery (if applicable)
  • Verify your beta access through your game library or platform

Missing Rewards?

If earned rewards are missing, they can be restored up to 14 days after the program ends. Contact Activision support with your account details and proof of participation.

Eligibility & Requirements

Who can participate and what accounts are needed

+

Participation Requirements

All players who meet the following criteria are eligible to participate in the Black Ops 7 Early Access Beta program:

Required Accounts:

  • Activision Account - Free to create if you don't have one
  • Twitch Account - Must be linked to your Activision account
  • Gaming Platform Account - Connected to Activision (PlayStation, Xbox, Battle.net, Steam)

Account Setup:

  1. Create accounts if you don't already have them
  2. Link your Twitch account to Activision
  3. Ensure your gaming platform is connected to Activision
  4. Verify all connections are active before participating

Note: YouTube integration may also be available for certain regions and promotions. Check official Activision communications for the most current platform support.
